\section{Introduction}
This formula represents the forward pass of a dense layer using the dot product:

\[
\begin{bmatrix}
w_{11} & w_{21} \\
w_{12} & w_{22} \\
w_{13} & w_{23} \\
\end{bmatrix}
\cdot
\begin{bmatrix}
i_1 \\
i_2
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
(w_{11} \times i_1) + (w_{21} \times i_2) \\
(w_{12} \times i_1) + (w_{22} \times i_2) \\
(w_{13} \times i_1) + (w_{23} \times i_2)
\end{bmatrix}
\]
